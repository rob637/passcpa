## Video Numbering System

```
CISA-[Domain]-[Type]-[Sequence]

Domain: D1, D2, D3, D4, D5, EX (Exam)
Type:   OV (Overview), CO (Concept), FW (Framework), 
        SC (Scenario), CP (Comparison), MA (Memory Aid), ES (Exam Strategy)
Sequence: 01, 02, 03...

Examples:
- CISA-D1-OV-01 = Domain 1 Overview
- CISA-D5-CO-07 = Domain 5 Concept #7
- CISA-D2-FW-01 = Domain 2 Framework #1
- CISA-EX-ES-01 = Exam Strategy #1
```
