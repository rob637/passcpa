**Strengths:**
- 42% have examples (industry-leading)
- 6 different card types (definition, rule, comparison, formula, concept, mnemonic)
- Balanced difficulty: 28% easy, 52% medium, 20% hard
- Average back length: 250 chars (good depth)
- 69% use question format (clear recall cues)

**Issues:**
- 43 duplicate fronts (7% - need deduplication)
- 20 vague fronts (too short/unclear)
- 7 truncated backs (data issue)

**Sample Quality:** ✅ Good
```
FRONT: What is INDEPENDENCE in auditing?
BACK: Both INDEPENDENCE OF MIND (state of mind permitting opinion...) 
      and INDEPENDENCE IN APPEARANCE (avoiding facts that would cause
      a reasonable observer to question independence).
EXAMPLE: Not owning stock in audit client (in fact) and not having 
         close family in client management (appearance).
```
