### Testing Windows

CPA exam is offered continuously, but common patterns:
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar):** Post-holiday surge, new year resolutions
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun):** After busy season for accountants
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep):** Summer studying
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec):** Before year-end, holiday slow period
