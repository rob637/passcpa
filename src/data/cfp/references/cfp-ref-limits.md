# 2026 Contribution Limits

## Retirement Account Limits

### IRA Limits

| Account Type | Under 50 | 50+ (Catch-up) |
|--------------|----------|----------------|
| Traditional IRA | $7,000 | $8,000 |
| Roth IRA | $7,000 | $8,000 |
| Combined IRA | $7,000 | $8,000 |

**Note**: Traditional and Roth IRA limits are combined (not additive)

## Roth IRA Income Limits (Phase-out)

### Single Filers

| MAGI Range | Contribution |
|------------|--------------|
| Below $150,000 | Full amount |
| $150,000 - $165,000 | Phased out |
| Above $165,000 | None (direct) |

### Married Filing Jointly

| MAGI Range | Contribution |
|------------|--------------|
| Below $236,000 | Full amount |
| $236,000 - $246,000 | Phased out |
| Above $246,000 | None (direct) |

**Backdoor Roth**: Still available regardless of income

## Health Savings Account (HSA)

| Coverage Type | Contribution Limit | 55+ Catch-up |
|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Self-only | $4,300 | $5,300 |
| Family | $8,550 | $9,550 |

### HSA Eligibility
- Must be enrolled in HDHP
- Cannot be enrolled in Medicare
- Cannot be claimed as dependent

### 2026 HDHP Requirements

| | Self-only | Family |
|---|-----------|--------|
| Minimum Deductible | $1,650 | $3,300 |
| Max Out-of-Pocket | $8,300 | $16,600 |
