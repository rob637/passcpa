# IIA Standards Summary

## Overview

The International Standards for the Professional Practice of Internal Auditing are mandatory guidance for internal auditors. They consist of:
- **Attribute Standards** (1000 series) - Characteristics of organizations and individuals
- **Performance Standards** (2000 series) - Nature of activities and quality criteria

---

## Attribute Standards (1000 Series)

### 1000 - Purpose, Authority, and Responsibility

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Internal Audit Charter | Formally defines purpose, authority, responsibility |
| Approval | By the board |
| Review | Periodically, at minimum annually |
| Content | Mission, independence, scope, responsibilities |

---

### 1100 - Independence and Objectivity

#### 1110 - Organizational Independence
- CAE reports functionally to the board
- CAE reports administratively to senior executive
- Board approves: IA charter, budget, CAE appointment

#### 1111 - Direct Interaction with the Board
- CAE must communicate and interact directly with the board

#### 1112 - CAE Roles Beyond IA
- Safeguards required for additional roles
- Independence may be impaired

#### 1120 - Individual Objectivity
- Impartial, unbiased attitude
- Avoid conflicts of interest

#### 1130 - Impairment to Independence/Objectivity
- Disclose impairments
- Consider organizational and individual factors

---

### 1200 - Proficiency and Due Professional Care

#### 1210 - Proficiency
- Knowledge, skills, competencies
- IT proficiency required
- Specialized services may need external expertise

#### 1220 - Due Professional Care
- Apply prudence and competence expected
- Consider needs and expectations of clients
- Consider complexity and materiality

#### 1230 - Continuing Professional Development
- Enhance knowledge and skills
- Includes CPE hours

---

### 1300 - Quality Assurance and Improvement Program (QAIP)

#### 1310 - Requirements
- Internal assessments (ongoing + periodic)
- External assessments (at least every 5 years)

#### 1311 - Internal Assessments
- Ongoing monitoring (part of daily work)
- Periodic self-assessments

#### 1312 - External Assessments
- Qualified, independent assessor
- Every 5 years minimum
- IIA Peer Review standard

#### 1320 - Reporting on QAIP
- CAE reports to senior management and board
- Report on conformance and improvement

---

## Performance Standards (2000 Series)

### 2000 - Managing the Internal Audit Activity

#### 2010 - Planning
- Risk-based audit plan
- Consider organization's risk management framework
- Input from senior management and board

#### 2020 - Communication and Approval
- CAE communicates plans and resource requirements
- Significant interim changes communicated

#### 2030 - Resource Management
- Ensure resources are appropriate, sufficient, deployed effectively

#### 2040 - Policies and Procedures
- Establish policies and procedures for IA activity

#### 2050 - Coordination and Reliance
- Share information and coordinate with other assurance providers

#### 2060 - Reporting to Senior Management and the Board
- Report on audit activity's purpose, authority, responsibility
- Report on plan and performance

---

### 2100 - Nature of Work

#### 2110 - Governance
- Assess and recommend improvements
- Ethics and values, performance management, accountability

#### 2120 - Risk Management
- Evaluate effectiveness of risk management
- Assist in establishing/improving process

#### 2130 - Control
- Evaluate adequacy and effectiveness
- Based on assessment of risks

---

### 2200 - Engagement Planning

#### 2201 - Planning Considerations
- Objectives, scope, timing, resource allocation

#### 2210 - Engagement Objectives
- Preliminary assessment of risks
- Objectives must reflect assessment results

#### 2220 - Engagement Scope
- Sufficient to achieve objectives
- Consider systems, records, personnel, physical properties

#### 2230 - Engagement Resource Allocation
- Determine appropriate and sufficient resources

#### 2240 - Engagement Work Program
- Achieving engagement objectives
- Approved prior to implementation

---

### 2300 - Performing the Engagement

#### 2310 - Identifying Information
- Reliable, relevant, useful information

#### 2320 - Analysis and Evaluation
- Appropriate analyses and evaluations

#### 2330 - Documenting Information
- Working papers support conclusions and results

#### 2340 - Engagement Supervision
- Appropriately supervised

---

### 2400 - Communicating Results

#### 2410 - Criteria for Communicating
- Include objectives, scope, conclusions, recommendations
- Must be accurate, objective, clear, constructive, complete, timely

#### 2420 - Quality of Communications
- Accurate, objective, clear, concise, constructive, complete, timely

#### 2430 - Use of "Conducted in Conformance..."
- May use statement only with QAIP support

#### 2440 - Disseminating Results
- Communicate to appropriate parties

#### 2450 - Overall Opinions
- Include: scope, limitations, considerations, opinion

---

### 2500 - Monitoring Progress

- Establish follow-up process
- Monitor management's response

### 2600 - Communicating Acceptance of Risks

- When management accepts risk that CAE believes is unacceptable
- Discuss with management first
- If not resolved, report to board
