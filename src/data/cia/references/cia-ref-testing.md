# CIA Exam Testing Information

## Exam Overview

The Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) exam is a three-part computer-based exam administered by Pearson VUE on behalf of The IIA.

---

## Three-Part Structure

| Part | Title | Questions | Time |
|------|-------|-----------|------|
| Part 1 | Essentials of Internal Auditing | 125 MCQ | 2.5 hours |
| Part 2 | Practice of Internal Auditing | 100 MCQ | 2 hours |
| Part 3 | Business Knowledge for Internal Auditing | 100 MCQ | 2 hours |

**Note**: Each part includes unscored pretest questions embedded in the exam

## Passing Score

- **600 out of 750** on scaled score
- Score is scaled based on difficulty
- No penalty for guessing

---

## Testing Availability

### Continuous Testing
- Available year-round at Pearson VUE centers
- No testing windows - schedule when ready
- Weekdays and some weekends available

### Appointment Scheduling
1. Register with The IIA (CCMS system)
2. Receive authorization to test
3. Schedule with Pearson VUE
4. Test within 180-day window

---

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Apply with The IIA
- Create CCMS (Certification Candidate Management System) account
- Complete application
- Pay application fee

### Step 2: Obtain Authorization
- Receive Authorization to Test (ATT)
- Valid for 180 days
- Must schedule and sit within window

### Step 3: Schedule with Pearson VUE
- Find testing center
- Select date/time
- Complete scheduling

## Retake Policy

| Situation | Policy |
|-----------|--------|
| Failed attempt | 60-day waiting period |
| Same part retake | Pay exam fee only |
| Multiple failures | No limit on attempts |
| Time limit | 3 years to complete all parts |

## Score Release

- Preliminary pass/fail shown at testing center
- Official score report within 3-5 business days
- Access through CCMS

---

## Testing Center Policies

### What to Bring
- Two valid forms of ID (primary with photo + signature)
- Names must match IIA registration exactly

### What's Provided
- Scratch paper/note board
- On-screen calculator
- Tutorial before exam

### Prohibited Items
- Cell phones and electronics
- Study materials
- Food and drinks
- Personal items (stored in locker)

---

## Exam Day Tips

1. **Arrive 30 minutes early**
2. **Use the tutorial** - get comfortable with interface
3. **Pace yourself** - don't spend too long on any question
4. **Flag and return** - mark difficult questions
5. **Answer everything** - no penalty for guessing

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with Part 1** - foundational for Parts 2 and 3
2. **Know the Standards** - critical for Parts 1 and 2
3. **Part 3 is different** - more business knowledge focus
4. **Practice MCQs extensively** - exam format matters
5. **Don't rush** - 3 years is enough time
