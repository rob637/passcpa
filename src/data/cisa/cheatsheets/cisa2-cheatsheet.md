## IT Governance Frameworks

### Key Frameworks
| Framework | Focus |
|-----------|-------|
| COBIT 2019 | IT governance & management |
| ISO 38500 | Corporate IT governance |
| ITIL 4 | IT service management |
| Val IT | IT value delivery |
| Risk IT | IT risk management |

### COBIT 2019 Focus Areas
1. **EDM** - Evaluate, Direct, Monitor (Governance)
2. **APO** - Align, Plan, Organize
3. **BAI** - Build, Acquire, Implement
4. **DSS** - Deliver, Service, Support
5. **MEA** - Monitor, Evaluate, Assess

## IT Governance Principles

### Five Focus Areas
| Area | Key Question |
|------|-------------|
| Strategic Alignment | Does IT support business goals? |
| Value Delivery | Does IT deliver promised benefits? |
| Resource Management | Are resources optimized? |
| Risk Management | Are IT risks managed? |
| Performance Measurement | Is IT performance tracked? |

### Governance vs Management
| Governance | Management |
|------------|------------|
| What to do | How to do it |
| Board/executives | Management |
| Direction setting | Execution |
| Evaluate, Direct, Monitor | Plan, Build, Run |

### IT Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Board | Oversight, strategic direction |
| CEO | Overall accountability |
| CIO | IT strategy & delivery |
| CISO | Information security |
| IT Steering Committee | Prioritization & oversight |

### RACI Matrix
| Letter | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **R** | Responsible (does the work) |
| **A** | Accountable (one person, final authority) |
| **C** | Consulted (input before decision) |
| **I** | Informed (notified after decision) |

### Segregation of Duties
Critical Separations:
- Development ↔ Operations
- Security ↔ Development
- Authorization ↔ Custody ↔ Recording

## IT Policies & Standards

### Policy Hierarchy
```
Policies (WHAT - mandatory rules)
    ↓
Standards (HOW MUCH - specific requirements)
    ↓
Guidelines (RECOMMENDED approaches)
    ↓
Procedures (HOW TO - step-by-step)
```

### Policy Lifecycle
1. Draft
2. Review
3. Approve
4. Communicate
5. Enforce
6. Monitor
7. Update

|--------|-------------|

| **Mitigate** | Reduce likelihood/impact |

## Exam Tips - Domain 2

1. **Governance = Board/Executives, Management = Execution**
2. **COBIT 2019 is the primary framework reference**
3. **Risk appetite comes from business, not IT**
4. **One person must be ACCOUNTABLE (A in RACI)**
5. **IT strategy must align with business strategy**
6. **SLAs must be measurable and enforceable**
