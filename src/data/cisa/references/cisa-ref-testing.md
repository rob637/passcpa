# CISA Exam Testing Information

## Exam Overview

The Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) exam is administered by ISACA through PSI testing centers worldwide.

---

## Exam Format

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Questions | 150 multiple-choice |
| Time Allowed | 4 hours (240 minutes) |
| Passing Score | 450 out of 800 scaled |
| Testing Mode | Computer-based |
| Languages | Multiple languages available |

---

## Testing Availability

### Continuous Testing
- Available year-round at PSI testing centers
- Schedule when you're ready
- No designated testing windows

### Scheduling
1. Register with ISACA
2. Schedule with PSI
3. Test within 12-month eligibility period

## Eligibility Requirements

### Experience Required
- **5 years** of professional IS audit, control, or security experience
- Experience must be in relevant domains

### Substitutions Allowed

| Substitution | Credit |
|--------------|--------|
| Bachelor's degree | 1 year |
| Master's degree | 1 year |
| University instructor (2 years) | 1 year |
| Other certifications (CISM, CIA, CPA) | 1-2 years |

**Maximum substitution**: 3 years (need 2 years actual experience)

## Exam Day Tips

### Before the Exam
1. **Get good sleep** the night before
2. **Eat well** - 4 hours is a long time
3. **Arrive early** - 30 minutes before
4. **Know your center** - visit beforehand if possible

### During the Exam
1. **Read carefully** - questions can be tricky
2. **Pace yourself** - 1.6 minutes per question average
3. **Mark for review** - flag difficult questions
4. **Answer everything** - no penalty for guessing
5. **Don't second-guess** - first instinct often correct

### Time Management
| Questions | Time Target |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1-50 | 80 minutes |
| 51-100 | 80 minutes |
| 101-150 | 80 minutes |

## Post-Exam Certification

### After Passing
1. Apply for certification
2. Submit experience verification
3. Agree to Code of Ethics
4. Pay maintenance fees

### CPE Requirements
- 20 CPE hours annually
- 120 CPE hours per 3-year cycle
- Report through ISACA portal
