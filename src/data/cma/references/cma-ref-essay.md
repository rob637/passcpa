# CMA Essay Writing Guide

## Essay Basics

### Exam Weight
- Essays comprise **25%** of each CMA part
- 2 essay scenarios per part
- Each scenario has multiple requirements

### Time Management
- 4 hours total exam time
- Recommended: **1.5 hours** for essays
- ~45 minutes per essay scenario

---

## Essay Scoring

### Grading Approach
Essays are graded by IMA-trained evaluators on:
- Technical accuracy
- Completeness of response
- Communication clarity
- Organization

### Partial Credit
- **Always attempt** every requirement
- Points awarded for correct concepts even if incomplete
- Show your work and reasoning

---

## The STAR Framework

Use this structure for maximum points:

### S - State the Issue
- Restate what's being asked
- Show you understand the question

### T - Technical Content
- Apply relevant concepts
- Use correct terminology
- Reference standards if applicable

### A - Analysis
- Apply concepts to the scenario
- Calculate as needed
- Explain your reasoning

### R - Recommendation
- Provide clear conclusions
- State your recommendation
- Support with evidence

## Writing Techniques

### DO:
- ✅ Use bullet points for lists
- ✅ Label calculations clearly
- ✅ Define terms before using
- ✅ Address all requirements
- ✅ Write in complete sentences
- ✅ Double-check numbers

### DON'T:
- ❌ Skip requirements even if unsure
- ❌ Write one long paragraph
- ❌ Ignore the scenario facts
- ❌ Copy the question in your answer
- ❌ Leave calculations unexplained

## Sample Response Structure

**Requirement: Explain the advantages of Activity-Based Costing**

> **Activity-Based Costing Advantages**
>
> Activity-Based Costing (ABC) offers several advantages over traditional costing methods:
>
> 1. **More Accurate Product Costs**
>    - ABC traces costs to activities, then to products
>    - Reduces cross-subsidization between products
>
> 2. **Better Decision Making**
>    - Management can identify cost drivers
>    - Enables pricing decisions based on true costs
>
> 3. **Process Improvement**
>    - Highlights non-value-added activities
>    - Supports continuous improvement initiatives
>
> **Conclusion**: ABC provides more relevant cost information for management decisions, particularly in environments with diverse products and significant overhead costs.

## Calculation Essay Tips

### Show Your Work
```
Step 1: Calculate contribution margin
  $50 - $30 = $20 per unit

Step 2: Calculate breakeven units
  $200,000 ÷ $20 = 10,000 units

Step 3: Calculate breakeven revenue
  10,000 × $50 = $500,000
```

### Always Include:
- Formula used
- Numbers plugged in
- Final answer
- Brief interpretation

## Ethics Essay Framework

### When Ethics is Tested:

1. **Identify the issue**
   - What ethical principle is violated?
   - IMA Statement reference

2. **Apply IMA Standards**
   - Competence
   - Confidentiality
   - Integrity
   - Credibility

3. **Recommend action**
   - Internal reporting first
   - Escalation path
   - Resignation as last resort

## Exam Day Tips

1. **Read the entire scenario** before answering
2. **Outline your answer** before typing
3. **Watch the clock** - don't spend too long on one part
4. **Leave time to review** for typos and completeness
5. **Answer every requirement** - even partial answers count
