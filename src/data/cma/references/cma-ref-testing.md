# CMA Testing Windows

## Exam Overview

The CMA (Certified Management Accountant) exam is offered during three testing windows each year at Prometric testing centers worldwide.

---

## 2024-2026 Testing Windows

| Window | Dates | Registration Deadline |
|--------|-------|----------------------|
| **January/February** | Jan 1 - Feb 28 | Dec 1 (recommended) |
| **May/June** | May 1 - Jun 30 | Apr 1 (recommended) |
| **September/October** | Sep 1 - Oct 31 | Aug 1 (recommended) |

**Note**: Register at least 6 weeks before your preferred date

## Exam Format

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Time | 4 hours per part |
| MCQs | 100 questions |
| Essays | 2 essay scenarios |
| MCQ Weight | 75% |
| Essay Weight | 25% |

### Time Allocation Strategy
- MCQs: ~2.5 hours (1.5 min per question)
- Essays: ~1.5 hours (45 min each)

---

## Passing Score

- **360 out of 500** on scaled score
- Raw to scaled score conversion accounts for difficulty
- No penalty for guessing

---

---

## Retake Policy

| Situation | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Failed Part | May retake in next window |
| Same window | Cannot retake same part |
| Consecutive failures | No limit on attempts |
| Time limit | Both parts within 3 years |

## Registration Steps

1. **Join IMA** (recommended for discounts)
2. **Pay entrance fee** to IMA
3. **Pay exam fee** per part
4. **Receive authorization** from IMA
5. **Schedule at Prometric** within 12 months
6. **Complete within window**

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Schedule early**: Popular dates fill quickly
2. **Complete both parts**: Within 3-year window
3. **Use IMA resources**: Included with membership
4. **Practice essays**: Worth 25% - don't neglect
5. **Essay partial credit**: Even incomplete answers get points
