# NTS & Testing Windows Guide

## Notice to Schedule (NTS)

### What is an NTS?
Your NTS is your authorization to schedule and sit for CPA Exam sections. Without it, you cannot book an appointment at Prometric.

---

## NTS Validity Period

| Jurisdiction Type | Validity |
|-------------------|----------|
| Most States | 6 months |
| Some States | 9 months |
| Few States | 18 months |

**Check your specific state** - validity varies!

---

## NTS Contents

Your NTS includes:
- ✅ Your candidate ID number
- ✅ Exam sections authorized
- ✅ NTS expiration date
- ✅ Jurisdiction information
- ✅ Payment confirmation

---

## Re-applying for NTS

### If NTS Expires Unused
- Must pay section fees again
- May need to reapply depending on state
- No partial refunds for unused NTS

### After Failing a Section
- Apply for new NTS for that section
- Must wait until next testing window
- Pay section fee again

## Testing Center Policies

### What to Bring
| Required | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Two forms of ID | Primary must have photo & signature |
| NTS confirmation | Recommended but not required |

### Acceptable Primary IDs
- Valid passport
- Valid driver's license
- State-issued ID
- Military ID with photo

### Acceptable Secondary IDs
- Credit/debit card with signature
- Social Security card
- Any ID with name matching primary

## Scheduling Best Practices

### Book Early
- Popular dates fill quickly
- Morning slots often preferred
- Weekends in high demand

### Rescheduling
| Timeline | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| 30+ days before | Free |
| 5-29 days before | ~$35 |
| 1-4 days before | ~$35 |
| Same day/No show | Forfeit fee |

---

## Testing Windows 2024+

### Continuous Testing
- Test year-round (no blackout months)
- Exception: 10-day period around score release
- Check Prometric for specific dates

### Same Section Retake
- Cannot retake same section in same quarter
- Can take different sections while waiting

---

## Score Release Schedule

### Typical Pattern
- Scores released ~2-3 weeks after window closes
- Target release dates published by NASBA
- Some delays during peak periods

### How to Check Scores
1. NASBA CPA Candidate Portal
2. State Board website (varies)
3. Prometric account

## State Board Contact Tips

### Common Issues Resolved
- NTS not received
- Name discrepancies
- Application status
- Credit extensions

### Response Times
- Email: 3-5 business days
- Phone: Often immediate
- Mail: 2-4 weeks
