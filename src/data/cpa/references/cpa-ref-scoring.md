# CPA Exam Scoring Guide

## Passing Score
- **75** on a scale of 0-99 (not a percentage)
- Score is a scaled score, not raw percentage correct

---

## Testlet Difficulty (MCQ)

### Adaptive Testing
- **Testlet 1**: Medium difficulty (all candidates)
- **Testlet 2**: Difficulty adapts based on Testlet 1 performance
  - Strong performance → Harder testlet
  - Weaker performance → Medium testlet

### Scoring Impact
- Harder questions worth more points
- Don't worry if questions seem harder - it's a good sign!
- Pre-test questions (unscored) are sprinkled throughout

---

## Score Release

### Timeline
- Scores released approximately **2-3 weeks** after testing window closes
- Check NASBA CPA Candidate Portal or your state board

### Score Report Contents
- Pass/Fail status
- If failed: performance breakdown by content area
  - Stronger
  - Comparable  
  - Weaker

---

## 30-Month Window

### Rules
- All 4 sections must be passed within 30 months
- Clock starts when you pass your first section
- If section expires, must retake

### Strategy
- Start with your hardest section
- Build momentum with sections playing to your strengths
- Consider testing windows and score release timing

---

## Retake Policy

### Waiting Period
- Must wait until next testing window (same section)
- Can take different sections while waiting

### Same Quarter
- Cannot retake same section in same quarter
- Exception: If you fail, can retake in next quarter
