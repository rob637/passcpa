# CPA Exam Timeline

## Overview: Application to License

```
Education → Application → NTS → Schedule → Test → Score → License
   ↓            ↓           ↓        ↓         ↓       ↓        ↓
150 hrs     2-4 weeks   6 months   ASAP    4 hrs   2-3 wks   1-2 mo
```

---

## Step-by-Step Timeline

### 1. Education Requirements ⏱️ Varies
| Requirement | Hours |
|-------------|-------|
| Total Credits | 150 semester hours |
| Accounting | 24-36 hours (varies by state) |
| Business | 24-36 hours (varies by state) |
| Degree | Bachelor's or higher |

**Note**: Some states allow exam at 120 hours, license at 150

---

### 2. Application to State Board ⏱️ 2-8 weeks

**Process:**
1. Choose your state (consider mobility)
2. Submit application online
3. Pay application fee ($50-200)
4. Send transcripts directly from university
5. Wait for eligibility confirmation

**Documents Needed:**
- Official transcripts
- State application form
- Application fee
- Photo ID copy (some states)

---

### 3. Receive NTS (Notice to Schedule) ⏱️ 1-2 weeks

**NTS Contains:**
- Candidate ID
- Exam sections approved
- Payment confirmation
- Validity period (typically 6 months)

**Important:**
- NTS validity: **6 months** (most states)
- Cannot schedule exam without NTS
- Fee per section: ~$240 (varies)

---

### 4. Schedule at Prometric ⏱️ ASAP

**Booking Tips:**
- Schedule early for preferred dates
- Prometric centers fill up quickly
- Can reschedule with 24-hour notice

**Testing Windows:**
- Continuous testing year-round (as of 2024)
- Blackout periods around score releases

---

### 5. Take the Exam ⏱️ 4 hours

**Exam Day Checklist:**
- [ ] Two forms of valid ID (one with photo)
- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early
- [ ] Nothing else needed (provided at center)

**Exam Structure (per section):**
- Tutorial: 10-15 minutes
- MCQ Testlets: ~2 hours
- TBS Testlets: ~2 hours
- Break available (15 min optional)

---

### 6. Score Release ⏱️ 2-3 weeks

**Score Notification:**
- Check NASBA CPA Candidate Portal
- Some states email directly
- Score reports show performance by area

---

### 7. Ethics Exam ⏱️ Varies

**AICPA Ethics Exam:**
- Required by most states
- Self-study course (~$150)
- Open book, can retake
- Score required: Usually 90%

---

### 8. Experience Requirement ⏱️ 1-2 years

**General Requirements:**
- 1-2 years (varies by state)
- Supervised by active CPA
- Verification forms required

**Experience Types Accepted:**
- Public accounting
- Industry
- Government
- Academia (some states)

---

### 9. License Application ⏱️ 4-8 weeks

**Submit:**
- License application
- Experience verification
- Ethics exam certificate
- License fee ($100-300)

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Start early**: Application backlogs happen
2. **Study for NTS period**: Don't let it expire
3. **Schedule immediately**: Best dates go fast
4. **Build buffer**: Life happens
5. **Track your 30-month window**: Calendar reminders
