# Tax Due Date Reference Guide

## Individual Filing Deadlines

### Form 1040 - Individuals

| Return Type | Due Date | Extended Deadline |
|------------|----------|-------------------|
| Calendar year | April 15 | October 15 |
| Fiscal year | 15th of 4th month | 6 months after |
| Americans abroad | June 15 (auto) | October 15 |

**If due date falls on weekend/holiday**: Next business day

## Employment Tax Deadlines

### Form 941 (Quarterly)

| Quarter | Due Date |
|---------|----------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | April 30 |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | July 31 |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | October 31 |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | January 31 |

### Form 940 (Annual FUTA)
- Due: January 31
- Extended to Feb 10 if deposits made timely

### W-2s to Employees & SSA
- Due: January 31

### 1099s to Recipients
- Due: January 31

### 1099s to IRS
- Due: February 28 (paper) / March 31 (electronic)
- 1099-NEC: January 31 (no extension)

## Special Situations

### Combat Zone
- Filing deadline extended 180+ days

### Disaster Area
- IRS announces extensions by locality

### Death of Taxpayer
- Final return due April 15 of year following death
- Executor files on behalf of decedent

## Exam Tips

1. **Partnerships/S Corps before C Corps**: March 15 vs April 15
2. **K-1 timing**: Flows through to individual returns
3. **Extension = file, not pay**: Interest still accrues
4. **Fiscal year**: Same day of month, different month
5. **1099-NEC**: No filing extension available
