## Exam Tip: High-Frequency Forms

For the EA Exam, know these cold:
1. **1040** - Always tested
2. **Schedule C** - Self-employment
3. **1065/Schedule K-1** - Partnerships
4. **1120-S/K-1** - S Corps
5. **941** - Payroll taxes
6. **1099-NEC** - Contractor payments
