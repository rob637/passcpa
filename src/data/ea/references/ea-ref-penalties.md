# IRS Penalty Reference Tables

## Taxpayer Penalties

### Failure to File (FTF)

| Situation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Filed late, tax due | 5% per month, max 25% |
| Filed late, no tax due | None |
| 60+ days late | Lesser of $510 or 100% of tax due |

**Note**: FTF runs concurrently with FTP for first 5 months

---

### Failure to Pay (FTP)

| Situation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Unpaid tax | 0.5% per month, max 25% |
| On payment plan | 0.25% per month |
| After IRS notice of levy | 1% per month |

**Interest**: Federal short-term rate + 3%, compounded daily

---

### Estimated Tax Penalty

| Situation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Underpaid estimates | Interest-based penalty |
| Safe harbor (prior year) | 100% of prior year tax |
| Safe harbor (high income) | 110% if AGI > $150K |

**Exceptions**: Under $1,000 owed, met withholding requirements

---

### Accuracy-Related Penalties (IRC §6662)

| Type | Penalty Rate |
|------|--------------|
| Negligence | 20% of underpayment |
| Substantial understatement | 20% |
| Substantial valuation misstatement | 20% (40% if gross) |
| Transaction lacking economic substance | 20-40% |

**Substantial Understatement**: Greater of 10% of tax or $5,000

---

### Civil Fraud Penalty (IRC §6663)

| Situation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Fraud on return | 75% of underpayment |
| Fraudulent failure to file | 15% per month, max 75% |

**Burden of proof**: On IRS (clear and convincing evidence)

---

## Tax Preparer Penalties

### IRC §6694 - Understatement Penalties

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Unreasonable positions | Greater of $1,000 or 50% of fee |
| Willful/reckless conduct | Greater of $5,000 or 75% of fee |

**Standard**: Substantial authority (not more likely than not)

---

### IRC §6695 - Administrative Penalties

| Violation | Penalty (2024) |
|-----------|----------------|
| Failure to furnish copy to taxpayer | $60 per return |
| Failure to sign return | $60 per return |
| Failure to include PTIN | $60 per return |
| Failure to retain copy | $60 per return |
| Failure to file information return | $60 per return |
| Endorsing/negotiating refund check | $600 per check |
| EIC due diligence failure | $600 per failure |

**Maximum**: $30,000 per preparer per year (most violations)

---

### IRC §6713 - Disclosure Penalties

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Unauthorized disclosure | $250 per disclosure |
| Maximum per calendar year | $10,000 |
| Criminal penalty (knowing/reckless) | $1,000 + up to 1 year |

---

## Circular 230 Sanctions

### Monetary Penalties (Administrative)

| Conduct | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Incompetence | Up to $5,000 |
| Disreputable conduct | Up to $5,000 |
| Violation of standards | Up to $5,000 |

### Non-Monetary Sanctions

- Private reprimand
- Public censure
- Suspension
- Disbarment

## Exam Tips

1. **FTF > FTP**: Failure to file is more expensive (5% vs 0.5%)
2. **File even if can't pay**: Reduces penalties significantly
3. **Reasonable cause**: Defense against most penalties
4. **Written advice**: Protects preparer from penalties
5. **Good faith reliance**: Defense for substantial authority
